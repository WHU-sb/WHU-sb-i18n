WHU.sb is an open, transparent, and collaborative course evaluation and resource sharing platform for students and faculty at Wuhan University.

### Our Vision
We believe that the free flow of information benefits everyone. During the four years at Luojia Mountain, selecting courses often feels like a game of information asymmetry. WHU.sb aims to break down these barriers, helping every WHU student find the most suitable courses through authentic reviews and experiences shared by fellow students.

### Open Source & Collaboration
This platform is fully open source and maintained by the WHU student developer community. We adhere to the MIT License, encouraging transparency, sharing, and continuous technological progress.

### Core Features
- **Authentic Reviews**: Multi-dimensional course evaluations from WHU students.
- **Resource Navigation**: Quick access to common systems like the Registry and Library.
- **Academic Support**: Optimize your academic planning through data analysis and experience posts.
