## 1. Introduction

WHU.sb (Wuhan University Course Evaluation System, hereinafter referred to as "the Platform" or "We") is committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your information. By accessing or using this service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using the related services. This policy was revised on March 18, 2026.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

To provide you with high-quality course evaluation and sharing services, we collect the following types of information:

### 2.1 Information You Provide Proactively
- **User Dynamic Content**: Course evaluations, ratings, suggestions, uploaded materials, and comments you post on the platform. This information is typically visible to other users based on your privacy settings.
- **Personal Identity Information**: When you register through specific authentication methods (e.g., email or third-party login), we collect your username, Unique Identifier (UID), and background information you choose to provide, such as your school and major.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Access Logs**: Including your IP address, browser fingerprint, device model, operating system version, access time, and clickstream data. This data is used for performance tuning and preventing malicious attacks (e.g., DDoS protection).
- **Cookies and Local Storage**: We use browser technologies such as localStorage, sessionStorage, and IndexedDB to store your preferences, offline cache, and login status. This data is primarily stored on your local device. We do not proactively upload unrelated local files.

## 3. Verification and Third-Party Services

To ensure system security and filter spam, we integrate the following third-party services:
- **Cloudflare Turnstile**: Integrated to filter automated scripts. This service may collect browser performance metadata and telemetry information for risk assessment. For details, please refer to the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
- **Edge Delivery Network (CDN)**: To improve loading speeds, your requests may pass through distribution nodes of Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare, etc. These providers may record basic network communication metadata in accordance with their compliance statements.

## 4. Disclosure and Sharing of Information

1. **Non-discriminatory Disclosure**: We do not sell your private data to any third party.
2. **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose necessary information in cases of explicit legal requirements, cooperation with government investigations, prevention of cybercrime, or emergency situations involving the safety of WHU.sb and its users.
3. **Academic and Statistical Use**: We may use de-identified anonymous data for course popularity statistics or academic research. Such data cannot be traced back to a specific individual.

## 5. Personal Information Security

1. **Secure Transmission**: The platform enforces HTTPS/TLS encryption for all traffic to ensure the privacy of data during network transmission.
2. **Precautionary Measures**: We protect your information through firewalls, access audits, and data backups. However, please note that the internet is not 100% secure. We recommend you safeguard your account credentials.

## 6. Your Rights and Content Control

Under applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:
- **Access and Correction**: You can query and modify your dynamic content and account profile through the "Personal Center" or "Settings" page.
- **Right to Erasure**: You can delete published reviews independently. To cancel your account and clear associated data, please apply through our contact channels.
- **Withdraw Consent**: You can stop our data reading behavior by clearing your browser cache or logging out.

## 7. Children's Privacy

WHU.sb is primarily intended for university students and adults. We do not intentionally collect personal information from minors under the age of 14. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us for processing.

Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the revised privacy terms.
